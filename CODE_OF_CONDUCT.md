# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want SkillGuard to be a welcoming, respectful, and constructive open source project.

Participants are expected to contribute in ways that help others learn, collaborate, and ship reliable work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent, then work with evidence.
- Offer feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Welcome questions from contributors at different experience levels.
- Focus discussion on the code, the behavior, and the user impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Deliberate disruption of community discussions
- Publishing private information without permission
- Bad-faith security reports or intentionally misleading examples

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If someone’s behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive, contact the maintainers privately.
